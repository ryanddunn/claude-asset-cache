## Success Criteria

- The Technical Writing team has built and launched an operational publishing process that allows non-technical staff (e.g., support staff) to publish content to the GenAI knowledge base
- The process is managed by the TW team but execution is owned by the non-technical contributors — TW does not do the publishing, they govern the process
- The process ensures content is formal, curated, and meets quality standards before reaching the Knowledge Catalog
- At least one non-technical team has been onboarded to the process and has successfully published content through it
- The EVB code personal care knowledge concept has been used as the pilot use case to validate the process
