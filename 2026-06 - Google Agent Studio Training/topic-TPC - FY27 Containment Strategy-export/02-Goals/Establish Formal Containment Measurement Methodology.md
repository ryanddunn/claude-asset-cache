## Success Criteria

- A formal, written definition of "containment" is agreed upon and approved by Ryan, Vivek, and executive stakeholders
- Methodology specifies: what counts as a contained interaction, which channels are included, how total contact volume is calculated
- Baseline is established using the agreed methodology (current: 22% as of June 2026)
- Reporting process is automated or repeatable, not manual
- Methodology is documented and stored as a reference artifact for FY27 KPI accountability
