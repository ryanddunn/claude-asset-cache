## Success Criteria

- Solutions teams (across relevant WellSky product lines) understand what content is needed in the Knowledge Catalog and what "quality" means for AI grounding
- Each solutions team has a named content owner responsible for their product's knowledge accuracy
- A review and update process exists so that product changes are reflected in the Knowledge Catalog before they cause AI hallucination or incorrect responses
- At least one solution's knowledge content has been reviewed, validated, and flagged as "agent-ready"
