## Success Criteria

- Every new team member who joined CE or IT in the last 12 months has completed a structured technical onboarding specific to the GenAI program
- Onboarding covers: the full pipeline architecture, their role in the execution, the tools and platforms they are responsible for, and how their work connects to the 50% containment goal
- New members are not operating in ambiguity about what they are building or why it matters
- Onboarding is documented as a repeatable process for future hires
