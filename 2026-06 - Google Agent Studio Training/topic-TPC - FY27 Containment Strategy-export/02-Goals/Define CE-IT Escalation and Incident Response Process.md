## Success Criteria

- A documented escalation and incident response process exists that covers GenAI production issues spanning CE and IT ownership boundaries
- Process defines: who owns what, how issues are escalated between teams, SLA expectations for GenAI system availability, and how Ryan is notified for executive-level incidents
- Process has been reviewed and agreed to by both CE and IT leadership
- Process has been communicated to all team members who may need to invoke it
- The lack of clear ownership boundaries — a known organizational risk — has been formally addressed for GenAI production systems
