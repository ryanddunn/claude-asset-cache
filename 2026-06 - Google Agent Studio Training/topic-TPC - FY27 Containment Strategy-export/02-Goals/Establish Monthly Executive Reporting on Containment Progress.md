## Success Criteria

- A monthly reporting artifact (dashboard, slide, or memo) on containment progress is being produced and delivered to executive stakeholders
- Report is consistent in format so executives can track trends over time — not a different format every month
- Report is delivered without requiring Ryan to manually assemble data each time — the measurement process is automated or semi-automated
- Report is being received and acknowledged — not just sent into a void
- At least three consecutive monthly reports have been delivered on schedule
