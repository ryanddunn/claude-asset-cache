## Success Criteria

- A repeatable audit process exists to review and validate Knowledge Catalog content for accuracy, completeness, and AI-readiness
- Audit cadence is defined (e.g., quarterly or triggered by product releases)
- Content issues found during audit are tracked, assigned, and resolved within a defined SLA
- The process accounts for the cross-functional nature of content ownership — product, tech writing, and CE each have a defined role
- At least one full audit cycle has been completed end-to-end
