## Success Criteria

- A regular executive review cadence is established (at minimum quarterly) where containment progress is presented
- Ryan presents directly to at least one of: President, COO, or CFO at each review cycle
- Each presentation includes: current rate vs. target, what drove changes, what's at risk, and next milestone
- No executive is surprised by containment progress — they are consistently informed throughout FY27
- Reviews are documented and stored as evidence of executive engagement and KPI progress
