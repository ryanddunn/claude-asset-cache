## Success Criteria

- WellSky has a mechanism — even if imperfect — to determine which accounts are using which solutions at the enterprise level
- Ryan and Vivek can pull account-to-solution data without having to run per-solution reports and stitch them together manually
- This data is available in a form that can feed the Knowledge Catalog and inform personalized AI responses (e.g., knowing a client is on Personal Care vs. Home Health)
- IT and Solutions teams have been aligned on a path to close this gap, even if full resolution is a multi-year effort
- The absence of this data is no longer a blocker to launching initial containment capabilities
