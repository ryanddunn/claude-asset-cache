## Success Criteria

- Google Knowledge Catalog (graph database) is provisioned and integrated into the data pipeline
- Knowledge Catalog is receiving structured content from the relational database
- Content coverage is sufficient to support Gemini for CX responses across the most common support topics
- Knowledge Catalog is connected to both the Gemini for CX chat and voice Agent Studio environments as the grounding data source
- A quality review process exists to validate that Knowledge Catalog content is accurate and current before agents consume it
